One Piece

The Pirate King, Gol D. Roger, struck fear into the hearts of all who sailed the Grand Line with his unmatched strength and notorious reputation. However, his downfall at the hands of the World Government instigated a momentous shift in the world. Roger's final words divulged the existence of One Piece, the ultimate treasure that promised boundless riches, fame, and the coveted title of Pirate King, setting off the start of the Grand Age of Pirates.
